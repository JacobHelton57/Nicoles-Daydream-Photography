About Nicole

Hi! My name is Nicole. I am a rising senior and will be attending John Tyler and Bridge Academy in the fall. I am passionate about photography and capturing that dream-like shot justb for you! I've taken photography for two years, am presently an intern photographer on the Creative Team at my church, and overall have loved taking picctures ever since i could get my hands on a DSLR. I have primarily focused my skills on becoming the best portrait photographer I can become, but also enjoy photographing pet and nature shots on the side.

Ever since I was a little girl selling lemonade in my front yard, I have desired to create my own business. I have enjoyed many hobbies such as playing the piano, crocheting, making balloon animals, jewelry designing, dog training, and others from which I have created small businesses. Of all my business-based hobbies, photography has become my favorite by far, and I work quite often to stretch my abilities and learn the full capabilities of my camera.

My current camera is a Nikon D3300 and my favorite lens to use is a 50mm. I am saving up to eventually purchase a more portrait appropriate lens and many other camera ad-ons to create stronger, higher quality pictures. Until then, I believe in working with what you’re given until you reach your equipment’s max potential, and as a photographer, can learn no more with what you own.

As for my future career as a photographer, I do not know exactly what that looks like or what God’s plan is for me, but for now I strive to earn a little on the side and continue creating the DayDream pictures you always imagined.
